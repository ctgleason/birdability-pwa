Site Review Checklist
Thank you for completing a Birdability Site Review to submit to the Birdability Map. Documenting the
physical accessibility of birding locations helps ensure that birding truly is for everybody and every body!
Once complete, please visit birdability.org/map to submit.
Question numbers on this checklist correspond to the question numbers in the online Birdability Site
Review; not all questions in Part 1 are included on this printable checklist.
